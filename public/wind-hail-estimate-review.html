Wind & Hail Damage Estimate Analysis

Wind & Hail Damage Estimate Review provides loss-type-specific analysis for wind and hail damage claims. The system compares detected trades against storm loss expectations and generates observational findings about estimate structure.

This is NOT coverage interpretation. Storm loss expectations are observational patterns only, NOT statements about what is covered, required, or owed.

Wind vs. Hail Damage Patterns

Wind Damage Only

Commonly observed trades: RFG, SID, WIN, GUT, FRM

Pattern: Often includes structural trades (FRM) if wind was severe

Secondary damage: DRY, PNT if water intrusion occurred

Hail Damage Only

Commonly observed trades: RFG, GUT, SHT, SID

Pattern: Focus on exterior surfaces (roofing, siding, gutters)

Less common: Interior trades (unless hail broke windows/skylights)

Combined Wind & Hail

Commonly observed trades: RFG, SID, WIN, GUT, SHT, DRY, PNT

Pattern: Both exterior damage and potential water intrusion

Storm Damage Integrity Checks

  • Roofing without gutters: RFG trade detected but GUT not detected
  • Water intrusion without interior work: Roof damage but no DRY/PNT
  • Removal without replacement: Damaged materials removed but not replaced
  • Exterior damage without water barrier: Siding/roofing without underlayment
