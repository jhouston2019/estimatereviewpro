Insurance Estimate Review Tool

Insurance Estimate Review analyzes carrier-provided estimates (typically in Xactimate format) and generates observational findings about estimate structure, trade categories, and line item patterns.

This is NOT claim advocacy. This tool does not argue with carriers, interpret coverage, or provide negotiation assistance.

⚠️ What This Tool Does NOT Do

  • Does NOT negotiate with insurance carriers
  • Does NOT interpret policy coverage or exclusions
  • Does NOT determine what carrier "should" pay
  • Does NOT provide legal advice or claim strategy
  • Does NOT give pricing opinions on carrier estimates
  • Does NOT advocate or argue on your behalf

What You Receive

  • Xactimate Format Detection: Confidence score for carrier estimate format
  • Trade Categories Detected: Which trades are present in carrier estimate
  • Categories Not Detected: Which commonly observed trades are NOT present (observational only)
  • Line Item Observations: Zero quantities, structural patterns
  • Loss Type Comparison: How estimate compares to loss type expectations
  • Integrity Findings: Results of 8 integrity rule checks

Common Use Cases

Reviewing Carrier Initial Estimate

Upload the initial estimate provided by your insurance carrier. System detects trade categories present and not present. Observational findings only - no opinions on adequacy.

Comparing Multiple Estimates

Upload carrier estimate and contractor estimate separately. Compare trade categories detected in each. System does NOT determine which is "better" or "correct".

Understanding Estimate Structure

Upload carrier estimate to understand Xactimate format, trade codes, and line item structure. Educational/observational use only.

⚠️ Critical Disclaimers

This tool provides observational analysis only.

  • Categories not detected ≠ carrier error or under-payment
  • Integrity observations ≠ estimate is wrong or inadequate
  • This is NOT a determination of what you are owed
  • This is NOT coverage interpretation
  • This is NOT pricing analysis

For coverage questions, consult your policy. For negotiation help, hire a public adjuster. For legal advice, consult an attorney.
