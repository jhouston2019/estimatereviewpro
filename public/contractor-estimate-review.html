Comparing Contractor vs. Carrier Estimates

To compare estimates:

  1. Upload carrier estimate first - note trade categories detected
  2. Upload contractor estimate second - note trade categories detected
  3. Compare the two lists of detected trades

System does NOT determine which estimate is "correct" or "better." It only reports which trades are detected in each.
