Water Damage Estimate Analysis

Water Damage Estimate Review provides loss-type-specific analysis for water damage claims. The system compares detected trades against water loss expectations and generates observational findings about estimate structure.

This is NOT coverage interpretation. Water loss expectations are observational patterns only, NOT statements about what is covered, required, or owed.

Water Loss Expected Trades

For water damage losses, the following trade categories are commonly observed in estimates:

Required Trades (Commonly Observed)

DRY
Drywall
Almost always affected in water loss
PNT
Painting
Typically follows drywall work

Common Trades (Frequently Observed)

FLR
Flooring
Frequently damaged by water
TRM
Trim
Often affected at floor level
CLN
Cleaning
Standard in water losses
DEM
Demolition
Removal of damaged materials
INS
Insulation
Often wet and must be removed

Conditional Trades (Sometimes Observed)

CAB - Cabinets
CTR - Countertops
ELE - Electrical
PLM - Plumbing
TIL - Tile
WIN - Windows
DOR - Doors

Water Damage Integrity Checks

The system runs water-damage-specific integrity checks:

  • Drywall without paint: DRY trade detected but PNT trade not detected
  • Removal without replacement: Flooring removal without reinstall
  • Zero quantities: Line items with zero quantity but labor descriptions
  • Material without labor: Materials listed without installation labor

Output: Observations only. No judgments about what "should" be included.
