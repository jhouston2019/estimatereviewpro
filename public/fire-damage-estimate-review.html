Fire Damage Estimate Analysis

Fire Damage Estimate Review provides loss-type-specific analysis for fire and smoke damage claims. The system compares detected trades against fire loss expectations and generates observational findings about estimate structure.

This is NOT coverage interpretation. Fire loss expectations are observational patterns only, NOT statements about what is covered, required, or owed.

What This Tool Does NOT Do

  • Does NOT determine if fire damage is covered under your policy
  • Does NOT interpret exclusions (arson, intentional acts, etc.)
  • Does NOT provide opinions on cause or origin of fire
  • Does NOT determine smoke damage extent or coverage
  • Does NOT provide fire restoration advice

Fire vs. Smoke Damage Patterns

Direct Fire Damage

Commonly observed trades: DRY, PNT, FLR, TRM, DEM, FRM, RFG, ELE, INS

Pattern: Structural trades (FRM, RFG) often included with direct fire contact

Smoke Damage Only

Commonly observed trades: CLN, PNT, DRY (if smoke penetrated walls)

Pattern: Heavy emphasis on cleaning (CLN) and painting (PNT)

Soot Damage

Commonly observed trades: CLN, PNT, CAB, FLR

Pattern: Surface cleaning and repainting, less structural work

Fire Damage Integrity Checks

The system runs fire-damage-specific integrity checks:

  • Drywall without paint: DRY trade detected but PNT trade not detected
  • Fire damage without cleaning: Structural work without CLN trade
  • Removal without replacement: Burned materials removed but not replaced
  • Structural damage without framing: Major fire but no FRM trade
