- Mandatory items from expert report not in estimate
- Structural requirements not addressed
- **Financial Impact:** Clearly stated with range

**2. High-Priority Disparities**
- Quantity shortfalls vs expert directives
- Missing trades required by expert
- Dimension variances exceeding thresholds
- **Financial Impact:** Calculated with cost baseline

**3. Moderate Issues**
- Recommended items not included
- Conditional requirements
- Minor quantity differences
- **Financial Impact:** Estimated range

**Insurance vs Expert Report:**
- What expert says vs what insurance estimated
- Specific line items affected
- Trade-by-trade comparison

- Quantity deltas (SF, LF, EA)
- Room-by-room variances
- Source: DIMENSION

**Combined Disparities:**
- Issues confirmed by both expert AND dimensions
- Highest confidence deviations
- Source: BOTH
